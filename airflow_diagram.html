Valve states: All valves are 2-way normally-closed (NC) — open only when energized by the relay module.
Drying tower: SUPPLY + RETURN valves open → air circulates: fan → tower → dry boxes → fan (closed loop).
Regen tower: SUPPLY + RETURN valves closed, EXHAUST valve open → fan pushes air through heated desiccant bed → room (open loop).
Towers switch roles every ~6–8 hours (configurable). Only one heater runs at a time.
