# Hardware Store Shopping List

For building the two desiccant towers. All items should be at Home Depot / Lowe's.

---

## Plumbing Aisle

- [ ] **2" Schedule 40 black iron pipe nipple, 6" long — qty 2**
      (one per tower; pre-threaded both ends; look near black iron fittings)

- [ ] **2" black iron pipe end cap — qty 4**
      (two per tower; you'll drill and tap these for air connections)

- [ ] **1/4" NPT close nipple, steel — qty 4**
      (short threaded stub; goes into the tapped end cap ports)

- [ ] **PTFE thread seal tape (Teflon tape)**
      (you probably already have this; grab one if not)

---

## Hardware / Fasteners Aisle

- [ ] **27/64" drill bit**
      (pilot hole size for 1/4" NPT tap; check the drill bit wall)

- [ ] **1/4" NPT pipe tap**
      (for cutting threads in the end caps; look in the tap-and-die section;
       sometimes stocked, sometimes not — call ahead if you want to be sure)

---

## Electrical Aisle

- [ ] **PG7 cable glands — qty 2** (or rubber grommets ~6mm ID)
      (heater wire pass-through in end cap; look with conduit fittings)

---

## Adhesives / Sealants Aisle

- [ ] **High-temp RTV silicone — 260°C+ rated**
      (for sealing cable gland holes; Permatex Ultra Copper or J-B Weld
       High-Temp RTV; if they only have standard red RTV check it's rated
       260°C / 500°F — auto parts stores are more reliable for this one)

---

## Building Materials (or Plumbing)

- [ ] **Steel window screen mesh — small piece**
      (cut two ~2" circles per tower to keep alumina out of the fittings;
       a small offcut is fine; fiberglass screen also works)

---

## Skip These — Order Online Instead

| Item | Why |
|------|-----|
| Silicone tubing 1/4" ID × 1/2" OD, 15 ft | Hardware stores rarely stock silicone; PVC looks the same but can't handle 180°C |
| 1/4" NPT male to 1/4" barb fittings × 12 | Selection usually poor in-store; cheap on Amazon/McMaster |
| Activated alumina desiccant | Not a hardware store item |

---

## Notes

- The pipe nipples should be around $3–5 each; end caps $2–3 each.
- If they don't have pre-cut 6" nipples, they can cut and thread a longer piece for you at the store (pipe threading service is free or cheap at most big-box stores).
- If they're out of the 1/4" NPT tap, you can drill a slightly undersized hole and use a 1/4" NPT fitting with extra Teflon tape as a workaround — but a properly tapped thread is cleaner.
